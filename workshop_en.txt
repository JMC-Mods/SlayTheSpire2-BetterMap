[h1]Minimap[/h1]
[h2] 1. Overview [/h2]
Places a minimap on the left side of the map screen to show an overview of the entire map. You can adjust its position in the settings screen.

[url=https://github.com/JMC2002/SlayTheSpire2-BetterMap] GitHub Repository [/url]
[url=https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV14ScUzTEo2] Demo Video (Bilibili) [/url]
